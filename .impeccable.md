## Design Context

### Users

NutrAI is used by people who want to record meals consistently and understand nutrition without doing tedious manual work. Core users include general health-management users, dieters, and people with diet-sensitive conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, or allergies.

### Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and friendly. The interface should feel like a careful health companion: clear enough for daily use, warm enough to reduce anxiety, and restrained enough to feel credible.

### Aesthetic Direction

Keep the current Toss TDS inspired direction: light theme, soft neutral surfaces, clear hierarchy, green as the rare primary action/accent color, and simple card/list layouts. Polish should refine spacing, typography, alignment, and state clarity without making the product feel visually different.

### Design Principles

- Preserve the current product identity; polish details, not the whole visual language.
- Use existing tokens from `app/lib/theme/app_theme.dart` before adding new one-off values.
- Keep health information scannable, calm, and low-friction.
- Make interactive states and empty/error/loading states clear without extra explanation.
- Favor restrained, practical UI over decorative effects.
